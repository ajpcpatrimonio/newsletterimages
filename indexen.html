Dear colleague, 


We are contacting you from Asociación de Jóvenes Profesionales por el Patrimonio Cultural (AJPC).


Through this email, we would like to introduce you to this new Spanish association dedicated to the management, promotion, and protection of cultural heritage, but which keeps its international perspective by being part of the European network ESACH, European Students' Association of Cultural Heritage.


AJPC was born from the enthusiasm and dedication of the former members of the ESACH working group in Madrid. In the past, you have been part of the development of its activities and its history. Thanks to your support we can take this step forward and continue our development as young professionals in the field of cultural heritage management. 


We would like to invite you to continue to join us and be an active part of the AJPC's family. 


The creation of AJPC is the formalization of what until now has been an informal working group. Our aim from now is to use our experience to lay the foundations for a new, more professional, and forward-looking association. In the coming months, we would like to introduce you to the activities we are working on and encourage you to participate in them or even collaborate in the development of new ones that you think might be interesting to the association. Meanwhile, we leave at your disposal our Youtube channel so that you can relive some of the activities in which you were part of. We also remind you that you can subscribe to the channel if you do not want to miss anything.


Through our activity, we aim to become a reference for young professionals in the cultural heritage management field. AJPC presents, in a sector in which it is increasingly difficult to find fair and adequate opportunities for the extensive training of new professionals, the opportunity to be part of a horizontal, open, and democratic association, where each of its members has the opportunity to develop their skills and evolve as a professional. 


Also, thanks to being part of the European network ESACH, we give the opportunity to all those who wish to join to be part of a big family benefiting from all that it has to offer.


From AJPC we would like to thank you once again for having collaborated with us in the past and invite you to continue to do so in the future. That is why from now on, we have opened all our communication channels both on social networks and on our website and of course in this same email address. 

 

Best wishes from all the members of AJPC.
